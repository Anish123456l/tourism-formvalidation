Famous destinations

Muzhupillangadi Beach

Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach is a beach on the Malabar Coast in the state of Kerala in southwestern India. It is considered as the longest drive-in beach in Asia and is featured among the top 6 best beaches for driving in the world in BBC article for Autos.

st. Angelo Fort (Kannur Fort)

St. Angelo Fort is a fort facing the Arabian Sea, situated 3 km from Canannore, a city in Kerala state, south India
